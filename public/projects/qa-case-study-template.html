QA Case Study Template

Use this page as the shape for future case studies. Replace the placeholder text with real project, testing, and defect information only when verified.

Purpose

Turn QA work into recruiter-readable proof instead of a generic placeholder claim.

Format

Short sections, real artifacts, and clear responsibility boundaries.

1

System Under Test

Describe the product, module, platform, or workflow being tested.

2

Scope and Objectives

Define what this QA effort aimed to validate and what was in or out of scope.

3

Environment and Role

State the environment, tools used, and your actual contribution in the QA process.

4

Test Strategy

Explain the testing approach: exploratory, functional, regression, API checks, documentation review, or combined coverage.

5

Test Cases

Summarize the test cases or test areas that were prepared and what they were designed to verify.

6

Defects Found

List the most meaningful issues discovered, including symptoms and affected behavior.

7

Severity and Priority

Explain how the issues were assessed and which defects were most important to address first.

8

Regression and Retest Notes

Capture what was rechecked after fixes and how stability was confirmed.

9

Lessons Learned

Close with what this work taught you about testing, communication, and product quality.
